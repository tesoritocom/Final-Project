Musical & Performance Era

Discover my journey through musical hobbies, including piano, songwriting, and more. These activities were humbling and fun!

Piano

I gave up on art and moved to music because it looked cool. I convinced my dad to buy me an electric piano and joined the music club. Honestly, it was really fun! I learned lots of songs I wanted but often got frustrated when they were hard to play.

Rating: 8/10. Loved learning songs, but it was very time-consuming since I decided to play by ear instead of learning to read sheet music.

Songwriting Era

This was one of my most humbling hobbies. For some reason, during kindergarten, I felt super motivated to write songs and even showed them to my mom (which I hope she has forgotten!). The era ended abruptly after a history class assignment where we had to creatively explain the Mexican Revolution.

My friends and I decided to make an album because we thought we were rockstars. We wrote one main song, but the performance was a disaster: the track stopped working, and we had to sing it a capella, looking like religious ladies in church. The lyrics were kind of good, but the execution was unforgettable—for all the wrong reasons.

Rating: 2/10. It wasn’t completely bad because I worked with friends, but the experience was rough!
