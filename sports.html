Sports Era

Here’s a look at my adventures with different sports. Each one brought new challenges and fun experiences!

Basketball

I joined a basketball team in elementary school after my teacher said I was good. Turns out, she told everyone that because they needed more players.

Rating: 4/10. Risk of finger injuries is not fun!

Football

I got into football during the World Cup. Playing with friends was fun, but some men make the sport toxic.

Rating: 6/10. Great exercise but too much running for me.

Volleyball

I got into volleyball after watching the Olympics and *Haikyuu!!*. It’s harder than it looks, but super interesting!

Rating: 5/10. Tough but fun!

Swimming

My parents forced me into swimming classes for exercise. It was annoying when younger kids outpaced me, but I loved the water.

Rating: 4/10. Fun in non-competitive settings.

Dance

I joined a dance crew, but being the oldest made me stand out too much. Learning Kpop choreographies was a highlight.

Rating: 3/10. Fun but humbling!

Badminton

One of the sports I haven’t given up on! It’s super fun, and I hope to keep improving my skills.

Rating: 8/10. A favorite activity!
