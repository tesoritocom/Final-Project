Sleep Experiments

I read somewhere that sleep cycles range between 30 and 90 minutes, so I tried to test it myself. The results? Let’s just say it wasn’t exactly groundbreaking science!

Rating: 5/10. Fun to experiment with but not life-changing.

YouTube Channel

This is easily one of my most humbling experiences. I started an anime-dedicated channel about 10 years ago, and somehow it blew up to 5-6k subscribers. However, I shut it down after my school friends found out about it (and also because of copyright issues).

Rating: 2/10. Never again. Humbling, but I learned a lot about putting myself out there.
